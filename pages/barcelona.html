FC Barcelona's UEFA Champions League Victories

1991-92

  • Achievement: First UCL title (European Cup).
  • Final Score: Barcelona 1-0 Sampdoria (after extra time).
  • Notable Players:
    • Johan Cruyff (Manager)
    • Ronald Koeman (scored the winning goal in the final)
    • Andoni Zubizarreta
    • Michael Laudrup
    • Hristo Stoichkov

2005-06

  • Achievement: Second UCL title.
  • Final Score: Barcelona 2-1 Arsenal.
  • Notable Players:
    • Frank Rijkaard (Manager)
    • Ronaldinho
    • Samuel Eto'o
    • Thierry Henry
    • Carles Puyol
    • Xavi Hernandez
    • Andres Iniesta

2008-09

  • Achievement: Third UCL title.
  • Final Score: Barcelona 2-0 Manchester United.
  • Notable Players:
    • Pep Guardiola (Manager)
    • Lionel Messi
    • Samuel Eto'o
    • Gerard Piqué
    • Xavi Hernandez
    • Andres Iniesta
    • Dani Alves

2010-11

  • Achievement: Fourth UCL title.
  • Final Score: Barcelona 3-1 Manchester United.
  • Notable Players:
    • Pep Guardiola (Manager)
    • Lionel Messi
    • Xavi Hernandez
    • Andres Iniesta
    • Sergio Busquets
    • Gerard Piqué
    • David Villa

2014-15

  • Achievement: Fifth UCL title.
  • Final Score: Barcelona 3-1 Juventus.
  • Notable Players:
    • Luis Enrique (Manager)
    • Lionel Messi
    • Neymar Jr.
    • Luis Suárez
    • Ivan Rakitić
    • Sergio Busquets
    • Andrés Iniesta
