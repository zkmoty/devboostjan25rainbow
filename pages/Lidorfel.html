Atletico Madrid Club de Fútbol

Real Madrid, also known as Los Blancos, is one of the most famous and successful football clubs in the world. Based in Madrid, Spain, the club was founded in 1902 and has since become a symbol of excellence in the sport.

Key Achievements

  • 🏆 14 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 🏆 35 La Liga titles
  • 🏆 19 Copa del Rey trophies
  • 🏆 Numerous international and domestic accolades
